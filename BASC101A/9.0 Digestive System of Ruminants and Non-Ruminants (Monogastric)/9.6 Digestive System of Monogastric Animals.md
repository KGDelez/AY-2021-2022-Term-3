## Digestive system of Monogastric Animals
![[Pasted image 20220614165056.png]]
- "Monogastric" - refers to the structure of the stomach
- stomach has a simple structure with 1 single compartment
- Mouth -> pharynx -> esophagus -> stomach -> small intestine -> large intestine -> rectum -> anus

### Monogastric animals
- swine
- horses
- dogs
- rabbits
- fowl
